I am a Senior Research Scientist at Yandex Research, where I lead the Visual GenAI team. Currently, our primary interest is exploring visual generative models that unify multiple CV domains and other modalities, with a particular emphasis on their capabilities for downstream tasks. Equally important, we focus on efficiency, striving to make large visual generative models more accessible. Recently, we have also started a new direction in 3D modeling, with a special focus on building a highly realistic autonomous driving simulator.

In addition to my research, I organize the Russian-speaking research seminars in Generative CV and teach the Visual GenAI course at the Yandex School of Data Analysis.

**Previously:** I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science at HSE University in December 2024, advised by [Artem Babenko](https://scholar.google.ru/citations?user=2Kv3JP0AAAAJ&hl=en). In 2022, I was a research intern at Meta AI, where I worked on large-scale vector search and retrieval-augmented language models, advised by [Matthijs Douze](https://ai.meta.com/people/804301491840160/matthijs-douze/) and [Zeki Yalniz](https://ai.meta.com/people/274060152308398/i-zeki-yalniz/). Earlier, in 2019, I interned at ETH Zurich, working with [Vincent Fortuin](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=XBlrYTIAAAAJ) on generative modeling for missing data imputation.
